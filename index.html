Bio

Einav Peretz-Andersson is a Senior Lecturer and Researcher at the School of Engineering, Jönköping University, Sweden, and is also affiliated with the Jönköping International Business School Media and Management Center (MMTC). She also serves as Program Manager for the Master’s programme in AI Engineering.

Her research specializes in AI-driven organizational transformation, with a focus on AI adoption, data-driven decision-making, explainable AI, and AI governance structures in both public and private sector organizations. She examines how artificial intelligence and digital technologies reshape organizational strategies, roles, business models, and institutional frameworks, and how organizations manage the strategic, ethical, and operational challenges associated with large-scale AI implementation.

With a multidisciplinary background spanning engineering, innovation, and policy-oriented research, Einav combines academic rigor with practical relevance. Her work aims to support responsible, sustainable, and inclusive digital transformation, bridging research and practice to help organizations navigate the complexities of AI-enabled change.

Research interests

  • AI-driven organizational transformation and AI maturity
  • Explainable AI (XAI) and human-centered AI systems
  • AI governance, ethics, and responsible AI implementation
  • Digital transformation and data-driven decision-making
  • Ecosystem collaboration for digital and circular transitions
