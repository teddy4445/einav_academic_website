Research program

My research advances AI Transformation as a distinct form of organizational transformation integrating strategy, structure, processes, culture, people, and technology.

  • Trust and governance of AI in industry
  • AI-augmented decision-making for manufacturing resilience
  • AI literacy and responsible adoption in higher education

Primary research areas

  • Digital transformation, strategy making, and implementation
  • AI and organizational decision making
  • AI, new business models, and socio-economic impacts
  • User behavior in digital products and services markets
  • Strategy making in digital ecosystems and platforms
