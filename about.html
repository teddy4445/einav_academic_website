Einav Peretz-Andersson (PhD) is a Senior Lecturer and Researcher at the School of Engineering, Jönköping University, and Programme Manager for the MSc in AI Engineering. Her work focuses on how organizations move from experimenting with artificial intelligence to embedding it responsibly at scale.

Her research advances AI Transformation as a distinct form of organizational transformation, integrating strategy, structure, processes, culture, people, and technology. She studies explainable AI (XAI) as a foundation for trust and accountability, AI governance in public and private organizations, and AI-augmented decision-making in complex settings.

With a multidisciplinary background spanning engineering, innovation, and policy-oriented research, Einav works closely with industry, public sector actors, and international academic partners. Her work contributes to responsible, sustainable, and inclusive digital transformation across organizations and ecosystems.

Research focus

  • AI-driven organizational transformation and AI maturity
  • Explainable AI (XAI) and human-centered AI systems
  • AI governance, ethics, and responsible AI implementation
  • Data-driven decision-making in complex organizations
  • Ecosystem collaboration for digital and circular transitions
